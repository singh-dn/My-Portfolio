About Me

I'm a passionate, self-proclaimed designer who specializes in full stack development (React.js & Node.js). I love bringing the technical and visual aspects of digital products to life. UX, pixel-perfect design, and writing clear, performant code all matter deeply to me.

I began my journey as a web developer in 2015 and have continued to grow by embracing challenges and mastering modern technologies like Next.js, TypeScript, NestJS, TailwindCSS, Supabase, and more.

When I'm not in developer mode, you can find me engaging with indie hacker communities or exploring early-stage startups. Follow me on Twitter or GitHub to stay connected.
